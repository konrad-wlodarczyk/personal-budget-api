I utilized AI while working on this project.
I planned how the app should work and structured it myself (not entirely, still used online sources and forums for advice
and best practices, just not strictly AI). I used AI to help with setup stuff and debugging things that I am still learning
(Docker, setting up JaCoCo). AI was also utilized to debate on the architectural decisions made (to be 100% sure everything
is enterprise level and not Youtube calculator tutorial level). To be 100% sure code works (but also for practice)
I wrote over 70 tests (some of the scenarios were thought-up by AI). Basically most of the logic and decisions are mine,
AI saved me a lot of time fixing errors and validating decisions.
